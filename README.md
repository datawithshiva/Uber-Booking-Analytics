**Uber Booking Analysis** 🚖

**Project Overview**

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Uber booking data to uncover trends, patterns, and insights into ride-hailing operations. It focuses on key metrics such as booking volume, revenue, cancellations, and customer-driver ratings. By analyzing this data, we aim to understand peak demand hours, service performance, and overall operational efficiency.

**Key Features**

Booking Volume Analysis: Track total rides over time.

Revenue Insights: Analyze total revenue generated across different vehicle types and time periods.

Cancellation Analysis: Understand patterns in customer and driver cancellations.

Ratings Analysis: Evaluate customer and driver ratings to assess service quality.

Visualizations: Interactive charts and graphs for easy data interpretation.

**Data Description**

The dataset contains Uber booking records with the following columns:

Date & Time of booking

Booking ID

Booking Status

Customer ID

Vehicle Type (Auto, Prime Plus, Prime Sedan)

Customer Rating & Driver Rating

**Tools & Technologies**

Excel (for data cleaning & preprocessing)

SQL (for data analysis and business queries)

Power Bi ( for data visualization )

**Insights & Recommendations**

Identify high-demand periods and optimize driver allocation.

Reduce cancellation rates by analyzing patterns in customer and driver behavior.

Improve service quality based on rating trends.

Use revenue analysis to focus on profitable vehicle types or routes.
